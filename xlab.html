About us

The Xlab is part of the research platform BurgLabs at Burg Giebichenstein University of Art and Design in Halle, Germany. We explore the artistic potentials of robotics and artificial intelligence.

For regular updates feel free to follow us on Twitter and subscribe to our newsletter.

Also check out the work of our colleagues at the BioLab and SustainLab

To find out about current research go to XLab Research, project documentation is at XLab Projects
